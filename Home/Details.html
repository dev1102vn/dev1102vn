He seems to be normal as many other youngers at the first time you see him. But only after visiting his workshop with all his ink paintings and calligraphy, you will be surprised by his talent at that young age. Going to university of education with major Art, he has learnt by himself the art of ink paintings and calligraphy which was rarely chosen by other students because of its difficulty. Learning by all himself without teacher, it was really hard for him to practice but he has given all his love and passion for each step of his work.

What we’ll do

Watercolors offer a lot of possibilities for creative self-expression. It is an art form that creates artistic representations, usually by brush on paper, using pigments that are water-soluble. Simply the colored blocks are swiped with a wet brush and the pigment transfers itself to the watered brush and thence to the paper.
